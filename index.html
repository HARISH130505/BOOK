MULLINER NIGHTS

PG WODEHOUSE

Mulliner Nights is a collection of short stories by the humorist P.G. Wodehouse. It features recurring characters like the inimitable Lancelot Mulliner, a clubman with a penchant for telling outrageous stories about his relatives and friends. Expect the usual Wodehousean mix of witty dialogue, romantic entanglements, and gloriously eccentric characters in these tales.
